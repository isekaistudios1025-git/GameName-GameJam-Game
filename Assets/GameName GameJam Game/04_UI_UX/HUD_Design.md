## Overview

The HUD provides essential information to the player.

It should be minimal and non-intrusive.

---

## Core Elements

- Player health (optional)
- Enemy health (optional)
- Progress indicator (optional)

---

## Design Goals

- Keep UI minimal
- Avoid clutter
- Do not distract from gameplay

---

## Scope Constraints

- HUD is optional for MVP
- Focus on gameplay clarity first
