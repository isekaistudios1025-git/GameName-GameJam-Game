## Overview

Defines the minimum animation set required for all characters.

---

## Player Animations

Required:

- Idle
- Walk / Run
- Attack
- Hit reaction

Optional:

- Jump
- Special attack

---

## Enemy Animations

Required:

- Idle
- Walk
- Attack
- Hit reaction

Optional:

- Death

---

## Animation Timing

- Attacks should be fast and responsive
- Hit reactions should be short
- Avoid long animations

---

## Design Goals

- Clear motion
- Easy to read
- Supports gameplay timing

---

## Scope Constraints

- Keep animation count minimal
- Avoid complex animation blending
