## Overview

Sound effects provide feedback for player actions and combat events.

---

## Required Sounds

### Player

- Attack sound
- Hit impact sound

---

### Enemies

- Hit reaction sound
- Defeat sound (optional)

---

### UI (Optional)

- Button press
- Menu navigation

---

## Design Goals

- Sounds should be short and punchy
- Clearly communicate actions
- Match timing of gameplay events

---

## Scope Constraints

- Minimal number of sounds
- Reuse sounds when possible

---

## Notes

> “Even one good hit sound can transform combat feel.”
